About TechClimbers

TechClimbers is an organization dedicated to serving the community and beyond by encouraging and enabling a passion for technology.

A strong technical foundation is important for the future of our children and our community, and it is the goal of TechClimbers to support this need by providing opportunities for learning and working with technology in an open, hands-on environment.

In doing so, TechClimbers is engendering a supportive community of makers and providing a safe place for them to pursue their shared interests and learn how a technical proficiency can have a positive impact on their lives and on their community.

TechClimbers is the creation of Ricky Hussmann and Jeff Smith. Both WVU alumni and Morgantown residents, they are dedicated to the mission of having an impact on the place they call home. Ricky holds a Masters degree in Electrical Engineering and Jeff holds a Masters degree in Computer science.
